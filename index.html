Chickpeas

Chickpeas are an incredibly nutritous and versatile food. They contain a large amount of protein, fiber and some fat. They are a legume that offers a healthy amount of folate, manganese, iron and copper. They can be prepared in a number of ways. This website will list some facts about Chickpeas, provide nutritional inforamtion and provide contact information.

5 Science Backed Facts About Chickpeas

  1. First cultivated in the Middle East 7500 years BC
  2. Other names include garbanzo beans, a popular term in the US, bengal grams, egyptian peas, ceci beans and kabuli chana.
  3. These plants actually restore depleted soils and are powerful nitrogen fixing legumes.
  4. Ground chickpeas have been used as a coffee substitute since the 18th century and are still commonly used as a caffeine-free alternative today.
  5. Chickpeas come in a variety of different types and colours, such as black, green, red and brown.
